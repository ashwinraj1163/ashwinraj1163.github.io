About Markfest

IMS Ghaziabad (University Courses Campus) takes pride in announcing its Mega Inter-Institutional Marketing Carnival Markfest-2020 on 1st Feb, 2020

The Marketing Carnival will see a high energy and vibrance in the campus where the students from various institutes will unleash their creative talents to emerge as marketing wizards and strategists.

The Festive mood will be highlighted with the presence of Business Haat, Food Court and the other attractions along with LIVE RADIO & DJ, to maintain the effervescence of the bright youth.

Events:

  • Brand Booster (Framing Punchline)
  • Color It Green (Face Painting)
  • Color Your Brand (Digital Rangoli)
  • Eco Friendly Movement (New Product Launch)
  • Brand Lens (Quiz)
  • Business Haat
  • Mark Sense
